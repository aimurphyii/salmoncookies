Seattle's Famous Salmon Cookie

Wholesome and locally sourced...

Pat's Pesca-tisserie is a farm-to-table establishment, meaning you get the finest and freshest ingredients from the northwest's finest in every sweet bite of salmon cookies. Berries and dairy from Skagit and Whatcom county are the backbone of our scratch-made frosting. The only ingredients that aren't farm-fresh are the salmon! All of the salmon in Pat's cookies are wild-caught Alaskan salmon, brought home by local fishermen.

From our family to yours...

Pat and his wife met and fell in love 15 years ago while baking cookies for a departmental fundraiser at UW. After graduation they worked in many capacties around the city involved with habitat restoration and sustainable development, and every night coming hoome to decompress over a warm oven. A few years ago they decided to take the leap, in the midst of a rapidly changing Seattle landscape, and decided to combine their two loves and create an establishment that they dreamed would represent Seattle heritage, fill stomachs, warm hearts, and give back to the community. On weekends you may even see their kids helping in the shop! Thanks for beign part of our dream!
